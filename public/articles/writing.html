Writing an Article

So you want to write an article for MachTarok.com

Great!

Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • There's no word limit - Write as much or as little as you like
  • Images are fine - but we have pictures of the cards. If you would like to have pictures of potential hands, include a note that says what cards
  • Don't worry about styling - We'll take care of that so you can focus on the content
  • Lists, bold, italics, and the rest - All of that is supported
  • Not sure what to write about? Check out the Topics page for inspiration
  • When your article is ready, email it to webmaster@smach.us with TAROKY ARTICLE as the subject line
  • Don't forget a title!
  • If you want to include a target audience and/or tags, you can (ex. target audience is new players, tags are Tarocky, Trump, Playing with Povinnost)
  • Please include your name, how long you have played taroky for, and what tournaments you have won
