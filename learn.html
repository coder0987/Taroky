How to Play Taroky

1: Beginning the Game
  1. The game begins when the host player presses 'Start Game'
  2. The server randomizes the deck of cards and chooses which player will start as dealer
  3. The server then prompts the dealer to shuffle
  4. The dealer may choose to shuffle the deck however many times, either randomly or algorithmically. The server shuffles the deck appropriately
  5. The dealer then informs the server the deck is fully shuffled
  6. The server passes the deck clockwise to be cut.
  7. That player may cut or call a special deal
  8. The server then returns the deck to the dealer, who may then deal (Auto-dealing will also be an option)
  9. During the first game at each table, whoever is dealt the II is povenost
  10. The server will reveal povenost and ask povenost to choose between prever or team play
  11. The server will continue counter-clockwise through each player and ask if povenost 'can go' or if that player would like to play prever

2A: Povenost (2v2) Play
  1. Povenost then draws 4 cards from the Talon. The 2 players immediately counter-clockwise from and across from povenost may either draw or pass their cards from the talon.
  2. The final player may refuse or accept a passed card.
  3. Povenost and each other player who drew cards must then discard down to twelve cards.
  4. Povenost then declares teammates (starting with the XIX in a standard game, no lower than XV allowed.)

Prever (1 v 3) Play

  1. Prever (the player who declared they would like to play by themself) draws 3 cards from the talon
  2. Prever may then lay those cards face up (in prever talon) and draw the remaining 3 cards or keep those cards
  3. If the cards were laid down, prever may return to them and lay the other 3 cards face-up in the prever talon
  4. Whichever cards were not kept are then discarded in the opposing team's discard pile.
  5. Prever must then discard down to twelve cards

Declaring Point Cards

  • 4: Big ones (10+ taroks)
  • 2: Little ones (8-9 taroks)
  • 2: Beda (1-2 taroks)
  • 4: Uni (0 taroks)
  • 2: Honery (pronounced pawn-e or ponnie, 4+ 5-point cards)
  • 4: Rosa-Honery (all 4 kings)
  • 6: Rosa-Honery+ (all 4 kings plus another 5-point card)
  • 2: Trul (Skyz, XXI/Mund, I/Pagat)
